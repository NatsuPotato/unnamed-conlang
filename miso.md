Content words are written in kanji

Function words and inflections are written in hiragana

Like Toki Pona, words can be nouns or verbs depending on context

```
食す / eat
食せす / ate
```

CV

`食 moku` / food (v. eat)

`月 yu` / night (v. sleep)

`日 mo` / day (v. wake up)

`行 kasae` / movement (v. go)

`語 miso` / language (v. speak)
